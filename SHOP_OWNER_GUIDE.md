### Handling Special Situations

#### Customer is Late for Pickup

**If order has been ready for 15+ minutes:**
1. Try calling customer (phone number in order details)
2. If no response after 30 minutes, mark as "Completed" with note
3. Consider a "held orders" area for late pickups

#### Missing Ingredients Mid-Order

1. Click order
2. Click "Contact Customer" (opens phone/SMS)
3. Offer substitution
4. Update order with substitution note
5. Proceed with preparation

#### Customer Requests Changes After Ordering

- **Before acceptance**: Can cancel and reorder
- **After acceptance**: Contact customer, explain changes aren't possible for accuracy
- **Offer**: Make it right with loyalty points or discount on next order

#### Wrong Order Made

1. **Remake the order correctly**
2. **Mark as Ready** when correct order is ready
3. **Add internal note** for training purposes
4. **Consider offering** loyalty points as apology

## Configuring Loyalty & Rewards

Build customer loyalty and encourage repeat visits with LoyalCup's integrated loyalty program!

### Accessing Loyalty Settings

**Dashboard â†’ Loyalty Settings** (or **Loyalty** in navigation)

**[Screenshot placeholder: Loyalty settings page]**

### Step 1: Enable Loyalty Program

1. **Toggle "Loyalty Program Enabled"** to ON
2. **Save Changes**

When enabled:
- Customers earn points on every order
- Your shop appears in loyalty searches
- Rewards are available for redemption

### Step 2: Configure Point Settings

**Points Earning Rate:**
- Set how many points customers earn per dollar spent
- **Default:** 10 points per $1
- **Range:** 1-50 points per $1

**Examples:**
- Conservative: 5 points per $1
- Standard: 10 points per $1
- Generous: 20 points per $1

**Formula:** Higher rate = faster rewards = more loyalty, but lower margins

> **ðŸ’¡ Tip:** Start with 10 points per $1 and adjust based on customer response and your margins.

**[Screenshot placeholder: Points configuration]**

### Step 3: Create Rewards

Rewards are what customers redeem their points for. Create compelling rewards!

#### Creating a Reward

1. **Click "Add Reward"**
2. **Fill out reward details:**

**Reward Information:**
- **Name** - Clear, enticing (e.g., "Free Medium Latte")
- **Description** - What they get (e.g., "Any medium latte of your choice")
- **Point Cost** - How many points needed (e.g., 250)
- **Expiration** - Days valid after redemption (e.g., 30 days)

**Redemption Settings:**
- **Max per customer** - Limit uses per customer (optional)
- **Total available** - Limited quantity (optional, e.g., 100)
- **Active dates** - Start and end dates (optional, for seasonal rewards)

3. **Click "Save Reward"**

**[Screenshot placeholder: Create reward form]**

#### Reward Ideas & Pricing

**Entry-Level Rewards (100-150 points):**
- Free pastry with purchase
- $2 off next order
- Free size upgrade

**Mid-Tier Rewards (200-300 points):**
- Free coffee (any size)
- Free specialty drink
- Buy one get one 50% off

**Premium Rewards (400-500 points):**
- Free bag of coffee beans
- $10 gift card
- Free lunch combo

**Point Pricing Guide:**
- At 10 points per $1, 100 points = $10 spent
- A $5 item at 200 points = customer spent $20 to earn reward
- Aim for 20-30% reward value vs. earning requirement

> **ðŸŽ¯ Strategy:** Offer a mix of low, medium, and high-point rewards to keep all customers engaged!

#### Managing Rewards

**Edit Reward:**
1. Click reward in list
2. Modify details
3. Save changes

**Deactivate Reward:**
1. Toggle reward to "Inactive"
2. Customers can no longer see or redeem
3. Previous redemptions still valid

**Delete Reward:**
1. Click "Delete" (trash icon)
2. Confirm deletion
3. Only if never redeemed

**[Screenshot placeholder: Rewards management list]**
