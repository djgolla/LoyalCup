## Getting Started

LoyalCup is available on two platforms:
- **Mobile App** (iOS & Android) - Best for ordering on-the-go
- **Web App** - Access from any browser at loyalcup.com

Choose the platform that works best for you! Your account, loyalty points, and order history sync across both.

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Steps

#### On Mobile App:
1. **Download** the LoyalCup app from the App Store or Google Play
2. **Open the app** and tap "Sign Up"
3. **Enter your information:**
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (for order notifications)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. **Accept** Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy
5. **Tap "Create Account"**
6. **Verify your email** by clicking the link sent to your inbox

#### On Web:
1. **Visit** loyalcup.com
2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. **Fill out the registration form** with the same information as above
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. **Check your email** for verification link

### Alternative Sign-Up Option

**Google OAuth Sign-In:**
- Click "Continue with Google"
- Select your Google account
- Authorize LoyalCup access
- Complete any additional profile information

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem:** "Invalid credentials" error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email and password are correct
2. Check for typos (email is case-sensitive)
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Try alternative login method (Google OAuth)
5. Clear browser cache/app data

---

**Problem:** "Email not verified"

**Solution:**
1. Check spam/junk folder for verification email
2. Request new verification link
3. Ensure email address is correct in profile

---

### Order Issues

**Problem:** Order stuck in "Pending" status

**Possible causes:**
- Shop is busy and hasn't accepted yet
- Shop may be experiencing technical issues
- Order submitted outside business hours

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 5-10 minutes for acceptance
2. Check shop hours and current status
3. Contact shop directly (see shop profile for phone)
4. Cancel and reorder if needed

---

**Problem:** Can't cancel order

**Explanation:** Orders can only be cancelled while in "Pending" status. Once accepted, contact the shop directly.

---

**Problem:** Wrong order or missing items

**Steps:**
1. Contact the shop immediately
2. Show your order confirmation
3. Shop will remake or refund
4. Report issue via app feedback (Profile â†’ Help â†’ Report Issue)

---

### Payment Issues

**Problem:** Payment declined

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check card has sufficient funds
3. Contact your bank (may be flagged as fraud)
4. Try alternative payment method
5. Update card expiration date

---

**Problem:** Charged twice

**Explanation:** Often a temporary authorization hold. Check:
1. Your order history (should show one order)
2. Wait 24-48 hours for hold to release
3. Contact support if charge persists

---

### Loyalty Issues

**Problem:** Points not showing up

**Timeline:** Points are awarded when order is marked "Completed"

**Solutions:**
1. Check order status (must be completed)
2. Wait 1-2 minutes after completion
3. Refresh the Rewards page
4. Verify shop participates in loyalty program
5. Contact support with order number

---

**Problem:** Can't redeem reward

**Checklist:**
- Do you have enough points?
- Is the reward still available?
- Has reward expired?
- Are you ordering from the correct shop?
- Is reward already applied to cart?

---

### App Technical Issues

**Problem:** App crashing or freezing

**Solutions:**
1. Force close and restart app
2. Check for app updates in store
3. Clear app cache (Settings â†’ Apps â†’ LoyalCup â†’ Clear Cache)
4. Restart your device
5. Uninstall and reinstall app
6. Ensure iOS/Android is up to date

---

**Problem:** Can't see shops or menu items

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Enable location services
3. Pull down to refresh
4. Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
5. Log out and log back in
